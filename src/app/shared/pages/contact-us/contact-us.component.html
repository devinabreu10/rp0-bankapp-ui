Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my online banking password?

You can reset your password by clicking the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You'll receive an email with instructions to create a new password. For security reasons, you may need to verify your identity through a one-time code sent to your registered phone number.

What should I do if I lose my card?

If you lose your card, immediately call our 24/7 customer service at (800) 123-4567 to report it. We'll deactivate your card to prevent unauthorized use and arrange for a replacement to be sent to your address. You can also freeze your card temporarily through our mobile app or online banking portal.

Can I contact support after hours?

Yes, our customer support is available 24/7 for urgent matters like reporting lost cards or suspicious transactions. For general inquiries, our regular business hours are Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 5 PM. You can also use our chatbot on the website or mobile app for assistance at any time.

How do I report a suspicious transaction?

If you notice a suspicious transaction, contact our fraud department immediately at (800) 987-6543. You can also report it through your online banking portal or mobile app by selecting the transaction and clicking "Report Suspicious Activity." Our team will investigate and take appropriate action to protect your account.
